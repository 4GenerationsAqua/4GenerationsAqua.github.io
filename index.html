Our Products

We will be offering 330ml, 500ml and 5 liter Packs initially.


The main product of the company is the bottled still water by the name of 4Generations Aqua. The main challenge facing the company in this bottled water industry is that there is no scope of invention and innovation in the product, which can be added as the additional benefits of the product. As for South African customers water is water only a medium to satisfy thirst, so any new innovation or value addition will not be able to generate curiosity among customer as well as not innovation is possible with the water.

Who we are: 

Le Mac Aqua Products (Pty)Ltd trading as “4Generations Aqua” company is a family business that is owned and managed by Len Valentine and his immediate family members, dad Leslie Valentine. Len Valentine is the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) and has experience in small scale local natural water production.

Len (46yrs old) has a background as Emergency Medical Practitioner (Intermediate Life Support) and returned to the farms two years ago to build his passion of becoming a farmer, in this case a natural bottled water farmer, and businessman which he has been pursuing over the past year. Len brings to the company his mentality of safety and will also for some part oversee the operational part aswell as manage the general business.

  Leslie (over 80yrs old), a retired teacher, layman architect and experienced farmer with exceptional business knowledge. Leslie brings with him his wealth of experience as farmer and business skills and his architectural background will manage the construction of the plant aswell.

Both will be working with a team of professionals to build and grow it to enviably height.

Our Business:

Our family owns a farm called Sandies Glen Farm, located just outside Stanford within the Agulhas Municipality, Western Cape. The farm has been in the family for over four generations and offers a good supply of natural spring water. The water on the farm has been tested and certified as natural spring drinking water suited for bottled water. We call our water “4Genereations Aqua” which shares a bit of our history and family legacy of owning this water rich farms for over four generations.

Much more than producing healthy, portable and well packaged bottled water, our customer care is going to be second to none. We know that our customers are the reason why we are in business which is why we will go the extra mile to get them satisfied when they purchase any of our bottled water and also to become our loyal customers and ambassadors.

Our Vision:

We believe we serve a greater purpose: Connecting people and improving their lives by providing people with an exceptional day to day experience from our family to theirs, knowing that just as our family over four generations have trusted and enjoyed the exceptional taste and quality of our family spring water supply, so can fellow South Africans through their “4Generations Aqua” bottled water experience each day.

We pride ourselves in service excellence all at an affordable price.
